About Me

I am a graduating senior from Trinity University. I have a B.S in Physics and Computer Science, with a minor in Mathematics and Music Performance. I have a deep background in theoretical and applied mathematics and physics, and enjoy using these to in my coding applications. I love working on original problems ranging from circuit design to AI chess bots, and will often use unorthodox and novel methods to solve them. I have research experience in Biophysics and Fluid Dynamics, as well as growing work experience in aerospace software, frontend/backend web development, machine learning, and data analytics.

In my free time, I run cross-terrain, make culinary experiments in my kitchen, play chess, compete in ultimate frisbee, read sci-fi/fantasy, and perform both solo and orchestral violin repertoire as the concertmaster of the Trinity Symphony Orchestra.
